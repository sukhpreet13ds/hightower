Hightower & Hightower, P.a.

Personal Injury Attorneys in Ocala, Florida

Since 1976, Daniel L. Hightower has been standing up for the rights of people injured in car, work, and other accidents. Hightower & Hightower, P.A.'s personal injury lawyers, workers' compensation lawyers, and social security disability lawyers have years of experience in successfully representing the interests of their clients and obtaining recoveries on their behalf.

The Law Office of Hightower & Hightower, P.A. has handled cases state-wide from Pensacola to Jacksonville to Islamorada to Tampa, Florida. We also proudly serve Alachua (Gainesville and surrounding cities), Bradford, Citrus, Clay, Columbia, Dixie, Flagler, Gilchrist, Hernando, Lafayette, Lake, Levy, Marion (Ocala and surrounding cities), Orange, Putnam, Seminole, St. Johns, Sumter (The Villages, Lady Lake and surrounding cities), Suwannee, Union, Volusia and other surrounding counties.
